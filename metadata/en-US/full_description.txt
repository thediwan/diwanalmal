Diwan Al-Mal is a privacy-first personal finance manager. All your financial data stays on your device in a local SQLite database — no cloud account, no tracking, no ads.

Features:
• Offline-first — works without internet
• Arabic-first RTL layout with full English support
• Multi-currency wallets with frozen exchange rates
• Income, expense, transfer, and debt tracking
• Monthly budgets and savings goals
• Monthly financial reports with PDF export
• Scheduled local backups (.dmbackup archives)
• Optional biometric app lock (PIN + fingerprint / Face ID)
• Light, dark, and system themes with color palettes

Your data belongs to you. Diwan Al-Mal is free software licensed under GPL-3.0-or-later.
